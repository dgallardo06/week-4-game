Pokémon Badge Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Pokémon badges below. By clicking on a badge you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lost the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each badge is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each badge.
